So What Is Torque?

#THE-PUSH-YOU-NEED Like the tagline suggests, Torque aims to be exactly the push needed to start it all up. Taking its participants through mock business pitch, product design and prototyping along with crisis management, the event provides well-rounded preparation over its three rounds. Making use of constructive criticism from judges in addition to the carefully curated resources issued, Torque is set to improve and encourage young innovators, especially women, to accelerate their growth in the sector and be well equipped for success.

Business Pitch

Time for your ideas to shine!

Marking the start of the competition, participants have to form an original idea for a business pitch. A pitch presentation with the intricacies of an execution plan, possible sources, finances, etc., has to be created and submitted by the end of the round. No limits of creativity are set-- even the most bizarre ideas are welcome, but with a plan to substantiate them. Here we aim to allow the participants to wrap their heads around their entire idea.

24th May 2021 - 28th May 2021

Design & Prototype

We need some stuff!

This round focuses on the material needs of the project. Participants have to create a prototype of the item or idea. Be it an outline of the UI of a website, a 3D model of the hardware or promotional material for social startups.

29th May - 30th May

Crisis-Management

It's time you get your brains roasted!

This round could be the most pertinent for those who wish to take their project to a level of actualisation. Here the participants will have a live Q&A with judges who will look into the plausibility of the pitch. In the end, each team will be given a crisis situation that they will have to manage at the moment
