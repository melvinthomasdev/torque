So What Is Torque?

#THE-PUSH-YOU-NEED Like the tagline suggests, Torque aims to be exactly the push needed to start it all up. Taking its participants through mock business pitch, product design and prototyping along with crisis management, the event provides well-rounded preparation over its three rounds. Making use of constructive criticism from judges in addition to the carefully curated resources issued, Torque is set to improve and encourage young innovators, especially women, to accelerate their growth in the sector and be well equipped for success.

Business Pitch

Time for your ideas to shine!

Marking the start of the competition, participants have to form an original idea for a business pitch. A pitch presentation with the intricacies of an execution plan, possible sources, finances, etc., has to be created and submitted by the end of the round. No limits of creativity are set-- even the most bizarre ideas are welcome, but with a plan to substantiate them. Here we aim to allow the participants to wrap their heads around their entire idea.

24th May 2021 - 28th May 2021

Design & Prototype

We need some stuff!

This round focuses on the material needs of the project. Participants have to create a prototype of the item or idea. Be it an outline of the UI of a website, a 3D model of the hardware or promotional material for social startups.

29th May - 30th May

Crisis-Management

It's time you get your brains roasted!

This round could be the most pertinent for those who wish to take their project to a level of actualisation. Here the participants will have a live Q&A with judges who will look into the plausibility of the pitch. In the end, each team will be given a crisis situation that they will have to manage at the moment

FAQ

Ask-Torque-Anything!

What are the kind of business ideas that we can pitch?
  • Basically anything under the sky that you view as a problem that you would like to solve
  • Any solution that can be represented as UI (for software) or 3D model (for hardware)
  • Even those with essentially no visual product, like social startups are qualified for TORQUE
  • An idea that can be extended successfully to the level of future growth and has a potential market.
    • Can we be completely creative or should it be fully practical?
  • Any idea is creative by default but a business idea needs practicality too
  • It should be creative in terms of solution, design etc but practical in terms of revenue profit and feasibility.
    • Are there any language restrictions while presenting?
  • Yes! Any business plan has a global factor to its market strategy. From discussing with people of different native tongue to talking to investors of different nationalities ENGLISH is the go to language.
  • Thus All the rounds in torque will be conducted in ENGLISH
    • When will the round wise details be given?
    A brief picture of the entire event, including the rounds, will be disclosed along with the event posters. However, the complete round wise details and guidelines will be given prior to each round.
    Will our ideas be disclosed to other participants?
    NO. your ideas won’t be disclosed to other teams.
    Which round does the customer review come in?
    Customer interviews can be done parallely and submitted at the end of the event which is the Third round. Final judgement is based on the analysis of the customer responses by which we can assess the details of how you intend to deliver a product or service.
    In the final report can we show improvements based on customer review?
    Yes you must. For the final report submission, we urge you to add genuine customer reviews to your business plan. Based on these reviews, you can definitely pitch in improvements. After all, the real test is not whether you make mistakes, it is whether you correct them.
    What are squads for?
    The participating teams are organized into a single tactical unit which are headed by volunteers.There should be a maximum of 4 participants in this squad. The purpose of a squad is such that teams within the squad question each other on their business plan/ model and we can scrutinize the best solution.
